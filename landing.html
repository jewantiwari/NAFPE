About NAFPE

The National Association of Nepalese Americans for Political Empowerment (NAFPE) aims to empower Nepalese Americans by increasing their civic consciousness and participation in community affairs and the mainstream political process. As a non-partisan and secular organization, NAFPE organizes programs, forums, events, and activities to promote political awareness and provide learning opportunities on civic, economic, business, and political issues. The organization also seeks to address legislative and regulatory developments affecting Nepalese Americans and facilitate exchanges of ideas between forum members and political leaders. NAFPE encourages naturalization, voter registration, civic responsibility, and involvement in community and public affairs.
